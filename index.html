Abbazia di San Galgano

Strada Comunale di S. Galgano, 53012 Chiusdino SI, Italy

We’re excited to share that we will be holding a small ceremony at the breathtaking San Galgano Abbey in Chiusdino, Tuscany. This roofless 13th-century Gothic cathedral is surrounded by rolling hills and cypress trees.

Guests will spend the weekend at The Farmhouse at Terre di San Galgano, a 3-minute walk from the Abbey, where all wedding festivities will be held.

Where to Stay

We are delighted to be covering accommodations for all guests at The Farmhouse on Friday and Saturday nights of the wedding weekend.

Your stay will include breakfast on Saturday and Sunday mornings.

If you’d like to extend your trip, there will be an option to book Sunday night at your own expense — perfect for those who want to soak up one more sunset in Tuscany before heading home.

How to Get There

The closest major travel hubs to Siena are Florence (FLR), Pisa (PSA), and Rome (FCO) airports. From there, Trenitalia offers easy and scenic train service to Siena — typically involving one transfer.

Travel times range from about 1.5 hours (from Florence) to 3–4 hours (from Rome). Tickets can be purchased in advance at Trenitalia or directly at the station.

We will be arranging shuttle transportation from Siena to our venue on Friday, October 9th, for anyone interested.
