We’re excited to share that we will be holding a small ceremony at the breathtaking San Galgano Abbey in Chiusdino, Tuscany. This roofless 13th-century Gothic cathedral is surrounded by rolling hills and cypress trees.

Guests will spend the weekend at The Farmhouse at Terre di San Galgano, a 3-minute walk from the Abbey, where all wedding festivities will be held.

Where to Stay

We are delighted to be covering accommodations for all guests at The Farmhouse on Friday and Saturday nights of the wedding weekend.

Your stay will include breakfast on Saturday and Sunday mornings.

If you would like to extend your stay through Sunday evening, please indicate this in your RSVP. We will share room options and pricing details via email based on your interest.

How to Get There

The closest airports are Florence (FLR) and Rome (FCO).

We recommend booking flights once schedules are available for October 2026. Most international arrivals into FCO land mid-morning to early afternoon, while FLR flights tend to be shorter regional connections.

From either airport, you will take a train to Siena via Trenitalia. While you may pre-book tickets, we recommend purchasing them upon arrival in case flight delays affect your timing.

If you would like assistance selecting train times, please submit your flight information via our travel form and we will gladly help coordinate the best option for your itinerary.
