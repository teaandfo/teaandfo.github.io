Abbazia di San Galgano

Strada Comunale di S. Galgano, 53012 Chiusdino SI, Italy

We’re so excited to share that we’ll be holding a small, intimate ceremony at the breathtaking San Galgano Abbey in Chiusdino, Tuscany. This roofless 13th-century Gothic cathedral is tucked in the Tuscan countryside - surrounded by rolling hills and cypress trees. Terre di San Galgano is a 3-minute walk from the Abbey and features a charming restaurant, pool, and walking paths.

Where to Stay

We are delighted to be covering accommodations for all guests at Terre di San Galgano for Friday and Saturday nights of the wedding weekend. This beautiful countryside property is just minutes from the Abbey, with rustic Tuscan charm and sweeping views. Your stay will include breakfast on Saturday and Sunday mornings. If you’d like to extend your trip, there will be an option to book Sunday night at your own expense — perfect for those who want to soak up one more sunset in Tuscany before heading home.

How to Get There

The closest major travel hubs to Siena are Florence (FLR), Pisa (PSA), and Rome (FCO) airports. From there, Trenitalia offers easy and scenic train service to Siena — typically involving one transfer. Travel times range from about 1.5 hours (from Florence) to 3–4 hours (from Rome). Tickets can be purchased in advance at www.trenitalia.com or directly at the station. On Friday morning, we will be arranging shuttle transportation from Siena to our venue, Terre di San Galgano, for anyone who would like to take advantage of it.
